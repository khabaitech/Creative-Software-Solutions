Features

some of our products features


User friendly design

All our systems were built with user friendly concept all way through. We try to keep the design as simple as possible so as to make the software easier to use.

Full of features

Our software comes with an extensive list of functions. For the price you are paying you are getting more functions than other competitors. Just to mention our software has its own accountancy module and event a payroll module.

Cloud based vs self hosted

You can decide to purchase the license of the software and the software is yours and you can install it at your premises. Or else you can pay a monthly fee and we host the software for you on the cloud.

Types of business

We have it all from pos software for restaurants, bars, takeaways, retail outlets, factories, and also pms software for hotels and tour operators. We also have ticketing software for museums, heritage sites, theme parks, tour operator software, saloon pos software and much more!

Bespoke Software Developement

Our software can be customized just for your business, not like other software which you have to use as it is. In our case we did a lot of customizations for various business including dedicate modules and screens which are not even available in the main version of the software.

Lifetime warranty and updates

Our software comes with a life time warranty on bugs and updates. You do not pay any annual subscription and we give you in return a life time warranty on bugs and free updates.
